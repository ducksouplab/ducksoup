Communication Test

The aim of this page is to test the communication setting to ensure that conference quality will be good. The test will assess the quality of your microphone as well as the functionality of your headphones and webcam.

1- Put your headphones on.
2- Choose your microphone and camera:
For the audio device, prefer the built-in microphone in your computer.
    • 3 - Instructions.

    At the start of the test, you will be told to remain silent for approximately 10 seconds. After this, a message saying 'Read the text below!' will appear. When prompted, read the text aloud in a normal pace and tone of voice. You should now be able to hear your own voice clearly.
    4 - Notice.

    If you pass the test it means you can participate in the experiment and will be given a code to proceed. Remember the settings you used for the test, you will be asked to use these during the experiment.

    If after pressing start you cannot hear yourself, not see yourself or if sound quality was bad, you are not allowed to participate in the experiment. Please return your prolific submission using this link. You will be paid for the time you took setting up the experiment.
